Since 1999 The Bakery has been making traditional food crafted by hand. We began in a small bakery making only one kind of bread and grew to our current The Bakery shop. Come by and try our freshly made breads, muffins, pies, cupcakes, and other seasonal treats.Our selection of baked goods includes classic European breads and pastries, as well as regional American favorites. We use organic flours and many other organic ingredients. All our products are made from scratch every morning on premise.
